Clarilux User Guide – Oral Board Exam Simulation
Phase 1: Account Creation and Onboarding
Step 1: Access the Platform
Open your preferred web browser and navigate to the application by typing
dev.clarilux.ai into the address bar. Press Enter to reach the landing page.

Step 2: Initiate Sign-Up
• As a new user, click the “Sign Up” button located at the top-right corner of the
page.
• If you are an existing user, please proceed to Log In. If you have forgotten your
password, click the “Forgot Password” link to reset it. Enter your email address
and submit the request. An email will be sent to you with instructions to create a
new password. Once your password has been successfully reset, proceed to log in
using your email address and new password.

Step 3: Choose Sign-Up Method
• You will be presented with options to sign up as a candidate, examiner, or
institution which would be candidate by default.
• Choose your preferred sign-up method: Google, Microsoft, or Email.

Step 4: Fill Out Account Details
• Complete the required fields, including your first name, last name, email address,
password, and confirm password.
• Check the box to agree to the Terms of Use, then click Create Account.

Step 5: Verify Your Email
• You will be redirected to a page confirming your account has been created and
prompting you to check your email.
• Log into your personal email account, locate the verification email, and click Verify
Email.

Step 6: Sign In
• After verification, you will be redirected to a page stating "Account has been
activated successfully" then click Sign In.
• Enter the email address and password you just created to log in.

Step 7: Complete Your Profile
• You will be prompted to provide more details about your role (e.g., Resident, Fellow,
Physician)

Step 8: Set Exam Timeline
• When asked "Have you taken this exam before?", select Yes or No.
• Input your anticipated exam date and click Submit.

Phase 2: Subscription and Starting the Session
Step 9: Navigate to Dashboard & Subscribe
• Click start preparing. You would be redirected to your dashboard
• To access the oral board sessions, you need an active monthly subscription
• Click on your profile at the top right corner, then select Billing and Subscription

Step 10: Plan Purchase

then click Subscribe.
• Once active, you will receive an email from Clarilux.

Step 11: Access Oral Board Sessions
• On the left-side menu (which includes Dashboard, Oral Board, Question Bank,
Marketplace, and Analytics), click on Oral Board.
• Choose a subject from the available options. E.g, Endocrine.

Step 12: Review Session Summary
• The screen will display a summary of the session, indicating it includes 2 cases, a
10-minute study period before each case, 35 minutes of case time, and 2 grab bags.
• Click Begin Session.

Step 13: Configure Session Experience
• While the first case is preparing, choose your experience: Avatar, Voice, or Text.
• Select your experience method and click Continue.

Step 14: Study Period & Session Start
• The case scenario will be displayed. You have a 10-minute review time to read the
question, draft your answers, and perform any necessary calculations.
• You can either wait for the 10 minutes to elapse (the session will start
automatically), or you can click Begin Early to jump straight into the active session.

Step 15: The First Case (Long Form or Short Form)

• The first session begins and the will last for exactly 35 minutes. This first case will
either be a long-form or short-form scenario.
• Once the 35 minutes elapses, you will be presented with a choice:
o Click Continue Later to pause the exam and immediately view your score for
this first case.
o Keep Going: Proceed directly to the second case without viewing your score
yet.

Step 16: Completing the Exam & Final Score
• If you choose to proceed to the next case, you will begin the second 35-minute
case.
• At the end of the full 70-minute exam time (35 minutes for the first case + 35
minutes for the second case), the session will conclude entirely.
• You will then be presented with your comprehensive score for both the long-form
and short-form cases.
• You can expand or collapse each session score accordingly.
• You can also view transcript of the entire session

Step 17: What’s Next
After viewing your score and transcript, you can choose to either return to the dashboard or
start a new simulation by clicking “Go to Dashboard” or “New Exam” accordingly.
• Go to Dashboard: Takes you back to the dashboard overview.
• New Exam: Takes you to the interface where you can select a topic and begin a new
